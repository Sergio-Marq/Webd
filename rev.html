Attack on Titan - Season 4

Epic battles and unexpected twists!

Review Summary

Attack on Titan's final season brings a stunning mix of emotional depth, breathtaking action, and complex storytelling. With the anime reaching its climax, fans are in for a ride with jaw-dropping twists and heart-wrenching moments.

Rating: 9/10

Season 4 brings the stakes higher than ever before. From Levi's intense battles to the brutal conflict between Marley and Paradis, the show is a masterclass in suspense and drama.

Review Details

  • Studio: MAPPA
  • Episodes: 16 (Part 1)
  • Genre: Action, Drama, Supernatural
  • Air Date: December 2020 - March 2021
