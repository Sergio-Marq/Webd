Forgot Your Login Credentials?

No worries! If you've forgotten your login information, follow the steps below to recover your account and get back to enjoying your services.

Forgot Your Username?

  1. Go to the Login Page: Navigate to the login page of our website or app.
  2. Click "Forgot Username": Below the login fields, click on "Forgot Username?".
  3. Provide Your Email Address: Enter the email address associated with your account. We’ll send you an email with your username.
  4. Check Your Email: Look for an email from us with the subject line “Your Username Recovery.” Follow the instructions in the email to recover your username.

Forgot Your Password?

  1. Go to the Login Page: Visit the login page of the website or app.
  2. Click "Forgot Password": Below the password field, click "Forgot Password?".
  3. Enter Your Email or Username: Provide either the email address or username associated with your account. We’ll send a password reset link to your email.
  4. Check Your Email: Look for an email with a password reset link. Click on the link, which will direct you to a page where you can set a new password.
  5. Create a New Password: Enter your new password and confirm it. Make sure your new password meets our security guidelines.

Still Having Trouble?

If you’ve tried the steps above but are still having trouble, don’t hesitate to reach out to our support team:

  • Email to our official email address.
  • Call us
  • Live Chat: Available on our website for instant support

We’re here to help you get back into your account as quickly as possible!
